CYSCOM

The Cybersecurity Student Community (CYSCOM) was formed in 2022 with the aim to spread awareness about cyber security and malware as well as ransomware threats in society and how to deploy counter measures through various events and undertakings.

We believe in maintaining a completely transparent and open source of code. Anyone, from anywhere can access it. We spread awareness regarding cybersecurity through weekly blogs, social media posts and more. With continuous projects and new ideas, we aim to create unique tools to spread awareness on cybersecurity.

Events

Over the years, CYSCOM has conducted a number of exciting and innovative events and competitions for spreading awareness of cybersecurity.

  • Cyber Defenders 2022
  • ICRTAC-2018
  • ICRTAC-2019
  • Outreach Programs
